Noun Cases

Greek nouns change their endings depending on their role in a sentence. There are 5 cases.

CaseRoleExample
NominativeSubjectThe God loves
GenitivePossessionof God
DativeIndirect objectto God
AccusativeDirect objectloves God
VocativeDirect addressO God!
