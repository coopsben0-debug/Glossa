Noun Case Inflection Mechanics

Unlike English, which depends on strict word arrangement patterns, Koine Greek changes letter suffixes to declare grammatical function context maps:

Case Form MatrixStructural Identity MappingTranslation Example
NominativeThe Subject or active driver executing the verb event.The brother speaks.
GenitivePossession value blocks, origin points, or descriptive modifications (translates with 'of').The word of God.
DativeIndirect object targets, location settings, or means indicators (translates with 'to/for/with').I write to the church.
AccusativeThe direct object absorbing transaction actions.He hears the message.
